1. James gets 20 lessons.
2. The first one is free, so he pays for 19 lessons.
3. He pays for every other lesson after the first 10, so he pays for 9 lessons.
4. Each lesson is $5, so he pays 9 * $5 = $45.

Q: Abby is building 2 raised beds to grow vegetables.  The beds are both 8 feet long, 4 feet wide and 1 foot high.  Each bag of soil has 4 cubic feet.  How many bags of soil will she need?
A:
1. The beds are 8 feet long, 4 feet wide and 1 foot high.
2. This means they are 8 * 4 * 1 = 32 cubic feet each.
3. She is building 2 beds, so in total she needs 2 * 32 = 64 cubic feet of soil.
4. Each bag has 4 cubic feet, so she needs 64 / 4 = 16 bags.
5. The answer is 16.
Answer: 16
